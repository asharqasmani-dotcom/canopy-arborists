Our Founder

The Story Behind Canopy Arborists

Edgar Guerrero spent over a decade working in arboriculture before founding Canopy Arborists with a clear purpose: to bring genuine professional standards and scientific rigor to tree care in the North Bay.

Having witnessed how trees are often mismanaged — over-pruned, misdiagnosed, or removed unnecessarily — Edgar built Canopy Arborists around a single commitment: that every tree deserves an evidence-based approach, not guesswork or shortcuts.

He holds an ISA Certified Arborist credential and the Tree Risk Assessment Qualification (TRAQ) from the International Society of Arboriculture — two of the most rigorous professional certifications in the field. His approach combines current arboricultural science with practical field experience accumulated across hundreds of projects in Sonoma, Napa, and Marin Counties.

Today, Canopy Arborists serves residential homeowners, HOAs, commercial properties, and municipalities — always with the same level of care, documentation, and professional integrity that defines the company's character.

How We Work

Core Philosophy

Three principles guide every decision we make and every tree we care for.

🌳

Preservation First

We exhaust all preservation options before recommending removal. Healthy trees are irreplaceable assets to your property and the broader urban forest ecosystem.

🔬

Science-Based Practice

Our work is grounded in current arboricultural research, ISA best management practices, and evidence — not tradition, habit, or guesswork.

🛡️

Risk Management

Clear, TRAQ-based risk assessments give property owners the information they need to make confident, defensible decisions about their trees.

What Drives Us

Our Values

The principles that shape how we work, how we communicate, and how we care for your trees.

🏭

Expertise

We invest continuously in professional development, certification, and staying current with the latest arboricultural science. Our credentials are not ornamental — they represent a genuine commitment to mastery of the field.

🤝

Integrity

We tell you what we find — not what you want to hear. Our assessments and recommendations are honest, unbiased, and never shaped by upselling. You can trust that our advice is in the best interest of your trees and your property.

🌿

Environmental Stewardship

Trees are a public resource, a climate asset, and a living part of the landscape. Every service we provide considers the broader ecological context — we care about the urban forest, not just the individual tree.
